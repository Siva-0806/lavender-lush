Our story

Every stitch carries a little bit of heart

Lavender Lush began in a small sunlit room with a single hook, a ball of lilac yarn, and a wish to make gifting feel personal again. What started as one woman's hobby has grown into a boutique loved by thousands who want their gifts to mean something.

Today, every bouquet, plushie and keepsake is still handmade to order by our small team of artisans — no factories, no shortcuts, just patient, careful craft.
